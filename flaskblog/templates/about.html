About

Welcome to the FF-BlogSpot, your home for high-octane, top-tier, and misguided fantasy football advice! "Three TE's, trust me, its a strat for 2020 PPR..." This podcast and blog is an independent fantasy football podcast that records and produces fantasy football content centered around ... well the fans. The show is focused on producing and developing content that is driven by the community, for the community!

The show will launch with the upcoming 2021 season, and will be hosted by John Cole. As a longtime gaming athlete and fantasy footabll trail blazer, I saw a need for a fresh take in the fantasy football universe, so I hiked up my try hard pants, combed my beard, and went to work.

If your looking for stat sheets and excel docs, then you've come to the wrong place. Yes we will discuss strategies, league formations and communication, and the sacrate day (draft day). But it's more than that, it's about winning the mental game, reading between the lines, finding the diamonds in the rough, and mocking your friends while you succeed.

Each episode can also be watched on YouTube. You can also connect with the show on a variety of social media destinations like Twitter and Instagram.

The die hard fans of the FF-BlogSpot are affectionately called #BSnation, a tight knit fantasy community. As a completely independent podcast without network backing, the show exists in part based on the support of the community as well as show advertisers. To learn more about supporting the show, email us at fantasyfootball.blogspot@gmail.com.
